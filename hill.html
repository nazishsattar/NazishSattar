Tusheti, Georgia




DETAILS

A 3 hour drive along one of the worlds most dangerous roads (or so says the BBC) you enter the center of the Caucasus, where Georgia borders Dagastan and Chechnya. For a region with such a bad image, the scenery is shockingly beautiful! Old slate villages are dotted on the mountains, with large towers among them to defend their border. Nowadays it's slowly turning into tourism towns, but you can still feel the old shepherd atmosphere and with a little luck you can see bears. This is a must go, stay at a locals place, enjoy their hospitality from wine, cheeses and Georgian bread... This is Sovjets Italy! .
